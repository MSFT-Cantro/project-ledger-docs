 Common Questions

Q: What's the difference between a quote and an estimate?  
A: Both use the same interface. Estimates support contingency amounts for contractor flexibility. You can convert between types anytime.

Q: Do old quotes still work?  
A: Yes! All existing quotes work exactly as before. New features are available for all documents moving forward.

Q: Where do I create a quote or estimate?  
A: Navigate to "Quotes & Estimates" in the main menu. Click "Create New" and select the type.

Q: Can I convert a quote to an estimate after sending it?  
A: Yes! Open the quote and click "Convert to Estimate." You can add contingency amounts during conversion.

Q: How do change orders work with estimates?  
A: Create a change order and link it to the estimate, just like you would with an invoice. Track all scope changes from proposal phase.

Q: Do clients see contingency amounts?  
A: You control what appears in the PDF and client portal. Configure visibility in settings.

Q: Where are the PDFs generated?  
A: Click "Generate PDF" from any quote or estimate. Download or email directly from the app.

Q: Can clients approve estimates in the portal?  
A: Yes! Clients can view estimates and interact with them through their portal access.

 Troubleshooting

Can't find quotes & estimates menu: Check navigation menu - it may be labeled "Quotes & Estimates" now instead of just "Quotes."  
PDF generation fails: Check your internet connection and try again. PDFs generate server-side.  
Convert button missing: Only certain document states allow conversion. Check document status.  
Client can't see estimate: Verify client has portal access enabled and document is marked for sharing.
