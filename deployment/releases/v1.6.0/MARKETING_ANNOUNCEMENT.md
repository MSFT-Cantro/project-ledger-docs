## üìÑ Contract Terms & Conditions Templates (Phase 2)
Protect your business on every job

Every contractor knows: good contracts protect everyone. Clear terms prevent disputes, manage client expectations, and give you legal protection when things go sideways.

But writing contract terms from scratch? That takes time you don't have. And using generic terms you found online? That's risky.

ProjectLedger v1.6.0 introduces a professional Terms & Conditions system with a library of contractor-focused templates. We've included standard terms for payment schedules, change procedures, warranties, liability, and more. Use them as-is or customize them for your specific business.

**Why you'll love it:**
- **Pre-Built Templates** ‚Äî Seven professional templates covering common contracting scenarios
- **Customizable** ‚Äî Modify templates to match your business policies and local requirements
- **Reusable** ‚Äî Save your customized terms and use them across multiple projects
- **Professional Protection** ‚Äî Written by professionals, covering the issues that matter

**What's included:**

**Standard Templates:**
1. **Payment Terms** ‚Äî Deposits, progress payments, final payment procedures
2. **Change Order Procedures** ‚Äî How scope changes are handled and approved
3. **Warranty Terms** ‚Äî What's covered, for how long, and what's excluded
4. **Liability Limitations** ‚Äî Protect yourself from unreasonable claims
5. **Project Delays** ‚Äî Weather, material shortages, and other delay clauses
6. **Material Specifications** ‚Äî Quality standards and substitution policies
7. **Dispute Resolution** ‚Äî Mediation and arbitration procedures

**How it works:**
1. Access the Terms & Conditions library from your settings
2. Browse pre-built templates or create your own
3. Customize terms to match your business practices
4. Attach terms to quotes, contracts, and invoices
5. Include them automatically in your PDF documents

**Real-world example:**  
A new client questions your payment schedule. Instead of explaining it verbally (and hoping they remember), you include your "Payment Terms" template in the quote. It clearly spells out deposit requirements, progress payments, and final payment procedures. No confusion, no disputes‚Äîjust professional documentation.

**Q: What changed with Change Orders?**

**Q: Can I edit the contract templates?**  
A: Yes! You can customize any template to match your business policies and local requirements. Save your customized versions for reuse.

### Troubleshooting

**Issue: Mobile interface looks the same as before**  
**Solution:** Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. The new layout is automatic but may require a page reload.

**Issue: Can't find Terms & Conditions templates**  
**Solution:** Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Terms & Conditions. The template library is in the main settings area.

**Issue: Change order edit button not showing**  
**Solution:** Only DRAFT and certain status change orders can be edited. Check the status. Approved change orders cannot be modified.

**Issue: Mobile layout seems broken**  
**Solution:** Ensure browser is updated to the latest version. Test in a different browser to isolate the issue.
