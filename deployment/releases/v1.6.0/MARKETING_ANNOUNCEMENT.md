# Project Ledger v1.6.0 - Release Announcement

**Release Date:** October 15, 2025  
**Version:** 1.6.0  
**Status:** Live in Production

---

## What's New in v1.6.0

We've rolled out Project Ledger v1.6.0 with updates focused on making your daily workflow faster and easier.

---

## Key Updates

### Faster Change Order Management
Managing change orders on the job just got simpler. We've streamlined the editing process so you can update project changes quickly, whether you're in the office or on-site.

**What's improved:**
- Quicker edits to change orders
- Consistent layout across all pages
- Better mobile access from the job site

### Better Mobile Experience
The app now works better on your phone and tablet, giving you more screen space to review quotes, invoices, and project details when you're out in the field.

### Contract Terms & Templates
Save time with pre-built contract terms and templates. Customize them for your business, or use our standard ones to protect yourself on every job.

---

## Who This Helps

This release is built for:
- **Contractors** managing multiple jobs with frequent changes
- **Small construction businesses** who need professional documentation
- **Trade professionals** working on-site who need mobile access
- **Business owners** who want better contract protection
